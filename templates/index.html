How the Smart Portal Works

Simple 4-step process connecting residents directly to municipal field teams.

1. Snap Photo

Take a photo of the uncollected waste or overflowing garbage bin.

2. Auto GPS Pin

Use one-click GPS detection or select the location on the interactive map.

3. Worker Dispatch

Smart Admin portal assigns the report to the nearest sanitation team worker.

4. Proof Verified

Field workers upload before and after proof photos once cleanup is completed.
